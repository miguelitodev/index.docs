## Grammar

### Adjectives
#### Short adjectives

To say if something is more than its we can add `er` in the end, like this..

adjective + er

> Cooler - cool
> Faster - fast
> Smarter - smart

#### Long adjectives

We dont have the er in the end, we need to say 

more + adjective

like:
> More intelligent 
> More creative
> More fascinating
